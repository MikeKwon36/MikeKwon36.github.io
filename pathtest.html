About

Basically anything related to Mobile & Web technology interests me. When I'm not working, you can find me coding on android studio, trading forex, planning my next run, or drinking too much coffee.

I received my MBA from NYU Stern, BA from the University of Rochester, and training in Android Development from Google's & General Assembly's Android Development Immersive.

Currently, I am a Technical Account Manager at Branch, which creates solutions for driving discoverability & sharing for mobile applications. In my role, I advise clients on implementing Branch's mobile & web sdk within their digital Strategy.

Prior I was Senior Client Partner at Citia, a web-based CMS for mobile content; a Management Consultant in IBM's Strategy & Analytics group; a Marketing Program Manager at AIG; and an Account Executive for Verizon FiOS' & Pfizer's advertising.
